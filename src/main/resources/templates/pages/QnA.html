Frequently Asked Questions

Questions about Donation

What type of donation do we recieve?

We accept giftable new items, gift cards, and monetary donations, all which we have Charity Tax Receipts available for. Click here for more information

Where do donations go?

We are collecting monetary donations or bottles to pay for some staffs, rent supplies, and equipment we will need to get going. Once we are set up, other fundraisers with our board and team of volunteers and apply for grants to keep funds coming and provide more support and programs.

Questions about Our Events

How do I enroll in the events?

You can click enroll from the events page.bAn account is needed for a reservation to be valid.

Will there be online events as well?

Yes! We are offering the opportunity to zoom into the class if they can leave cameras on and be present if childcare or other barriers comes up.

Other Questions

How to apply as a volunteer?

Please email us your CV on wellnesstruthfamilies@gmail.com. We will get back to you regarding further steps.

How to make an account?

Find the tab 'Login/Sign Up' on the top right of the page next to the Exit button. You will be redirected to the login page where the Sign Up button is available.

What is the Exit Button?

It is an emergency button that redirects to The Weather Channel. In the case where the web visitor, is in an unsafe environment and would not like to be seen browsing the website.
